Museum Policies & Amenities


Photo Policy

Photoshoots are not permitted. Visitors may use a cellular device to capture special moments, but no flash, stabilizers, or selfie sticks are allowed. To schedule a photoshoot in Plant Hall, contact University of Tampa Event Services.

Service Animals

Service Animals are welcome! However, we do not allow Emotional Support or Comfort Animals, as they are not considered service animals under the ADA guidelines.

Restrooms

Fully accessible restrooms are located off the main lobby on the first floor of Plant Hall, directly adjacent to the museum entrance.

Accessibility & Inclusivity

We strive to make the Henry B. Plant Museum welcoming and accessible for all our visitors.

Wheelchair Access

Accessible ramps are located at the north end and west side of the building. A free wheelchair is available on a first-come, first-served basis at the front desk. We are proud to partner with Wheel the World.

Closed Captioning

CC and multilingual subtitles (Spanish, French, German) are available on our 14-minute orientation DVD. Request this service at the front desk upon arrival.

Audio Guides

A complimentary self-guided audio tour is available in English and Spanish for your handheld device. Transcripts are also available in large print.

Language & Large Print

Informational brochures and DVDs are available in Spanish, French, and German. Large-print brochures and exhibit labels are available upon request.

White Glove Tours

Special "White Glove Tours" are available for visitors who are blind or partially sighted. Please contact us two weeks in advance to reserve.

Sensory Friendly

We welcome individuals with autism. Be aware the museum uses authentic 1891 dim lighting and plays soft classical music. View our Story Book to help plan your visit.
