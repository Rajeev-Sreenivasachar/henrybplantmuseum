Henry Plant: He's More Important Than You Thought

Live Performance

About the Event

The exhibit Henry Plant: He’s More Important Than You Thought honors the 200th anniversary of the visionary's birth by exploring his far-reaching legacy as a promoter, employer, and technological pioneer who revolutionized transportation and tourism across the Southeast.

Event Details

Dates: September 9, 2023 - December 23, 2023

Hours: 10:00 AM – 5:00 PM daily

Location: Henry B. Plant Museum, 401 W Kennedy Blvd, Tampa, FL 33606

Tickets:$20 for adults, $18 for seniors (65+), $14 for youth (4-18), and free for infants (0-3).

What to Expect

  • The exhibit highlights Henry Plant’s role as a promoter of Florida through curated displays and exhibits featured at World’s Fairs and international expositions.
  • It explores his transportation empire and his efforts as an employer, including innovations like hospital rail cars and medical care for workers.
  • Visitors will learn about Henry Plant’s embrace of new technology, showing how he integrated cutting-edge 19th-century innovations into his railroads and luxury hotels.
  • The display also examines his lasting legacy of infrastructure development, including the Waycross Short Line, steamship networks, and a system of grand hotels that transformed travel in the Southeast.

Visitor Info

Admission: $20 for adults, $18 for seniors (65+), $14 for youth (4-18), and free for infants (0-3).

Accesibility: Wheelchair accessible, with staff assistance available throughout the event.
