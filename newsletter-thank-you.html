Welcome to the Museum Society Newsletter

Check your inbox for your first Henry B. Plant Museum newsletter!

Thank you for signing up to stay connected with the Henry B. Plant Museum. You have successfully joined our inner circle of history enthusiasts, preservationists, and patrons.

Keep a close eye on your inbox for our exclusive monthly newsletters. You will be the first to receive curation insights, historic deep-dives into the Gilded Age, special announcements for upcoming exhibitions, and priority invitations to our live theatrical events.

Note: We send you our newsletter the second Sunday of each month. If you do not see our newsletters in your inbox, please go to your "All Mail" folder and drag our email to your inbox to ensure you receive all future updates. We can't wait to share the wonders of the Plant Museum with you!
