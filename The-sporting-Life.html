The Sportin' Life

Tampa's beloved holiday tradition


“Step back in time and experience the holiday magic of Tampa Bay during the Gilded Age.”

About the Event

The exhibit The Sportin' Life at the Henry B. Plant Museum explores the vibrant sporting lifestyle of America’s upper class at grand resort hotels from the 1880s through the 1930s.

Event Details

Dates: September 9, 2023 - December 23, 2023

Hours: 10:00 AM – 5:00 PM daily

Location: Henry B. Plant Museum, 401 W Kennedy Blvd, Tampa, FL 33606

Tickets:$20 for adults, $18 for seniors (65+), $14 for youth (4-18), and free for infants (0-3).

What to Expect

  • The exhibit explores the “sporting lifestyle” of America’s upper class from the 1880s to 1930s, highlighting how the wealthy spent their leisure time at grand resort hotels.
  • It showcases a variety of popular recreational activities of the era, from golf, lawn tennis, and baseball to croquet, archery, and shuffleboard.
  • Visitors will see vintage travel brochures, advertisements, and newspaper clippings that promoted luxury accommodations and elite sporting facilities such as the Tampa Bay Hotel.
  • The exhibit also highlights the history of professional baseball spring training in Tampa, including teams like the Chicago Cubs and the Washington Senators, and their connection to resort-era tourism and athletics.

Visitor Info

Admission: $20 for adults, $18 for seniors (65+), $14 for youth (4-18), and free for infants (0-3).

Accesibility: Wheelchair accessible, with staff assistance available throughout the event.
