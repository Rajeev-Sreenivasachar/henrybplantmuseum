Volunteer

Thank you for being interested in volunteering for our museum


Docent

Docents are knowledgeable ambassadors who share the Museum’s stories and interpretive approach with the public. They lead school field trips and adult tours, and are expected to demonstrate flexibility, responsibility, and respect toward visitors, staff, and fellow volunteers.

Store Asssistant

Store Assistants help manage the Museum Store during open hours. They assist customers with selecting and purchasing items, ring up merchandise, handle transactions, restock shelves, and keep the store clean and organized between visitors to maintain a welcoming environment.

Information

If you need more information for volunteering, please contact Todd Edwards at tedwards@ut.edu or 813.258.7304.
