The Exotic Architecture

Program by Curator/Registrar Susan Carter and former Curator of Education Heather Trubee Brown

Past Event

About the Event

This program is when Plant Museum Curators Susan Carter and Heather Trubee Brown spoke about the life and work of architect J.A. Wood. See photos of the Tampa Bay Hotel under construction, learn about the architec of this National Historic Landmark, and see other buildings designed by him.
