The Sexual Politics of French Operetta

The politics of French Operetta in Gilded Age America


About the Event

This film explores how French operetta became a cultural phenomenon in Gilded Age America. It highlights entrepreneur Henry Plant, who famously named his steamships after his favorite stage shows. The documentary shows how these satirical plays eventually evolved into the modern American musical. It also features rare, historically informed performances of songs that haven't been heard in decades.
